STORE POLICIES

Cancellation Policy

We aim to process and ship all orders as quickly as possible, which means cancellations are typically not possible once an order has been confirmed. Our team begins preparing items shortly after purchase to ensure timely delivery.

If an order remains unfulfilled, we will first reach out to our supplier to confirm its fulfillment status. In cases where an item is out of stock, we’ll offer an alternative product for your consideration. This gives you the opportunity to receive a similar item without delay.

Only if no suitable alternative is agreed upon will we proceed with cancellation and issue a full refund. Otherwise, all confirmed and available orders are considered final.

Delayed Orders

This Delayed Orders Workflow aims to effectively manage customer inquiries related to delayed shipments, minimize disputes by providing timely communication and compensation options, and ensure customer satisfaction through clear resolutions including refunds, discounts, or replacement shipments.

🔔 Important Note:

Always prioritize resending the item according to our policy instead of issuing a refund.

1. Initial Customer Outreach About Delay

Customer Message: Asks about order delay or delivery time.

  • Apologize for the inconvenience.
  • Inform delivery timeline:
    • Delivery Time: 5-12 business days
    • Processing Time: 1–3 business days
  • If order is within delivery timeframe: Ask customer to wait, reassure them order is on track.
  • If order is outside delivery timeframe: Offer up to 40% discount for a future purchase.

2. Customer Threatens to File a Dispute

Customer Message: Threatens dispute due to delay.

  • Acknowledge frustration and apologize.
  • Inform customer they will still receive the item.
  • Offer a 40% discount for a future purchase as a resolution.
  • Emphasize that the item will still be delivered.

3. Order Stuck in Transit (No Movement > 5 Days)

Condition: Tracking shows no update for more than 5 business days.

  • Potential lost in transit.
  • Contact supplier to know the status and confirm if lost.
  • Ask the supplier to resend order if confirmed lost.

4. Customer Communication After Confirmed Lost in Transit

  • Inform customer item is lost in transit and replacement is being sent.
  • Offer up to 40% discount for future purchase as apology and reassure that it will be resent.

Return Funnel - Customer Wants to Return

This return workflow is designed to provide alternative solutions to customers who wish to return their order for any reason other than damaged, incomplete, or incorrect items. The goal is to resolve issues efficiently—minimizing returns and operational costs—while maintaining customer satisfaction.

🔔 Important Note:

Always offer a replacement first as the primary resolution for return-related inquiries (excluding damaged, incomplete, or incorrect orders). Replacements are subject to a small fee based on the original order value. This provides customers with a quick, lower-cost solution while avoiding the inconvenience of return shipping.

📦 View Replacement Fee Breakdown
  • 30% of the item's price

1. Initial Customer Inquiry

Action: Customer reaches out expressing disappointment with the product (e.g., dresses don’t meet expectations).

Response:

  • Acknowledge the issue empathetically and apologize.
  • Offer 10% refund without return to resolve the issue quickly and conveniently.
  • Alternatively, offer free replacement if there is an issue with the dress.
  • Clearly mention that return shipping is the customer's responsibility (typically $25–$55 USD), and customs charges may apply depending on the location.
  • Recommend using tracked shipping for returns, as untrackable packages cannot be refunded.

We're truly sorry to hear that you’re not satisfied with the clothing, we always aim to delight our customers, and it’s disappointing to hear this wasn’t your experience. Before you go ahead with a return, we want to make sure you're aware of the significant costs and logistics involved. Since our items are made and shipped from our manufacturing partners overseas, returning them means international shipping and customs processing, and unfortunately, those fees add up quickly. Return costs typically include: $25 to $55 USD in transport charges $28 to $72 USD in customs clearance fees These can vary depending on the weight of your parcel, but in many cases, they end up being more than the refund itself. We’d love to offer you a simpler and more cost-effective option: ✅ Keep your item and get 10 percent cashback, our way of saying thank you for giving us a try ✅ If there’s a problem, we’ll send you a free replacement, no need to return anything, just send us a clear photo or video so we can help Many of our customers choose this hassle-free solution and find it to be the most convenient path forward. Please let us know how you’d like to proceed, we’re here to make this right for you.

2. Customer Requests Higher or Full Refund

Action: Customer requests a higher or full refund instead of the 10% offer.

Response:

  • Express understanding and appreciation for their feedback.
  • Offer 25% refund without return to resolve the issue in a more generous and quicker manner.
  • Alternatively, offer a 50% store credit on a future purchase as a gesture of goodwill.
  • Reiterate that return shipping costs (typically $25–$55 USD) will be the customer’s responsibility if they decide to return the item, along with any potential customs charges.
  • Emphasize the importance of tracked shipping to avoid issues with lost or untrackable returns.
  • Ask the customer to confirm if they would prefer the 20% refund without return or would still like to return the product for a full refund.

Action: Customer insists on a full refund and references a dispute, chargeback, or similar threat..

  • Make clear this is the maximum refund that can be offered without a return.

📋 Dispute Management: Customer Has Already Filed a Dispute / Chargeback

When a customer files a dispute or chargeback, follow these steps:

Coordination with Dispute Team

  • Verify order status (fulfilled, in transit, stuck, unfulfilled, etc.)
  • Understand the dispute reason
  • Apply the appropriate resolution strategy below

⚠️ Important:
Do not issue any refund or discount unless the customer has confirmed that the chargeback/dispute has been withdrawn.
Always request written confirmation and verify with the Dispute Team before processing any refund or discount.

📌 1. Order Is Lost or No Movement (>5 Business Days)

Status: No tracking update for more than 5 business days.

Steps:

  • Consider the item lost in transit.
  • Offer a full refund in exchange for withdrawal of the dispute/chargeback.
Customer Message Template ▼
Thank you for getting back to us. We understand you’ve filed a dispute and we sincerely apologize for the delay. It appears the item was lost in transit. We can process a full refund if you agree to withdraw the chargeback/dispute. Please confirm so we can proceed immediately.

⏳ 2. Order Is Still Within Delivery Timeframe

Status: Tracking is active and still within the 9–15 business day delivery window (after 1–3 business days processing).

Steps:

  • Reassure the customer the order is on the way.
  • Offer a 30% discount if they withdraw the dispute.
  • Customer keeps the item when it arrives.
Customer Message Template ▼
Thanks for getting in touch. Your order is still within our standard delivery timeframe and is currently on its way. As a goodwill gesture, we can offer you a 30% discount if you agree to withdraw the dispute. You’ll still receive your order as expected. Let us know how you'd like to proceed!

🚚 3. Order Is Outside Timeframe but Tracking Is Moving

Status: Order is delayed but still in transit with tracking updates.

Steps:

  • Confirm the order is still moving.
  • Offer a 30% partial refund in exchange for withdrawing the dispute.
Customer Message Template ▼
We’ve checked your order and although it’s delayed, it is still in transit. We can offer you a 30% partial refund if you agree to withdraw the dispute. You will still receive your order, and we appreciate your continued patience.

❌ 4. Unfulfilled Order (Not Shipped)

Status: Order was never fulfilled or shipped.

Steps:

  • Cancel the order immediately.
  • Offer a 30% discount code for a future purchase.
  • Process a full refund only if the customer withdraws the dispute/chargeback.
Customer Message Template ▼
We’ve reviewed your order and found that it has not yet been fulfilled. If you’re willing to withdraw the chargeback, we’ll be happy to process a full refund right away. As an additional apology, here’s a 30% discount code you can use on your next purchase: SORRY30. Please let us know how you'd like to proceed—we’re here to help!

💥 5. Damaged, Incomplete, or Incorrect Item Received (Second Resend)

Status: Customer received a damaged item—possibly twice—and filed or maintained a dispute.

Steps:

  • Apologize sincerely and acknowledge the issue.
  • If this is the first time, consider a resend, but for repeat damage, do not offer another replacement.
  • Offer a full refund in exchange for withdrawing the dispute.
  • Optionally include a 30% discount code for a future order.
Customer Message Template ▼
We're extremely sorry to hear that your item arrived damaged. We understand your frustration—especially if this has happened more than once. We’re prepared to issue a full refund if you're willing to withdraw the dispute/chargeback. As a token of apology, here’s a 30% discount code for your next purchase: SORRY30. Please let us know how you'd like to proceed.

📦 6. Delivered But Not Received

Status: Tracking shows “Delivered” but customer claims item was not received.

Steps:

  • Request Proof of Delivery (POD) from the supplier or courier.
  • Do not issue a refund at this point.
  • Offer a 30% discount only if the customer withdraws the dispute/chargeback.
Customer Message Template ▼
Thank you for reaching out. Our records show that your order was marked as delivered, but we understand you haven't received it. We're currently requesting proof of delivery (POD) from our shipping partner to investigate this further. In the meantime, we’d like to offer you a 30% discount as a goodwill gesture, if you’re willing to withdraw the dispute/chargeback. Let us know how you'd like to proceed—we appreciate your patience while we look into this.

🔧 7. Size Not Fit or Quality Issue

Status: Customer files dispute due to wrong size or quality dissatisfaction.

Steps:

  • Offer to resend the correct size or replace item if quality issue.
  • Offer a 30% discount as goodwill.
  • Request withdrawal of dispute in exchange for resend + discount.
Customer Message Template ▼
We're sorry to hear that the size didn’t fit / the quality did not meet your expectations. We’d like to resend the correct size or replace the item for you. Additionally, we can offer you a 30% discount as a goodwill gesture if you agree to withdraw the dispute/chargeback. Please let us know how you’d like to proceed.

🚫 Order Cancellation Workflow

1. Check Order Fulfillment Time

  • Cancel allowed only if order is out of stock.
  • If the order is still unfulfilled, DO NOT CANCEL. Instead, follow up with the supplier for fulfillment.

2. Cancellation Conditions (When cancellation can be processed)

A. Product Out of Stock

  • Always offer a replacement first.
  • If customer declines replacement, proceed with cancellation and refund.

B. Undeliverable Address

  • Request an alternative address from the customer.
  • If alternative address is also not serviceable, contact supplier to request a refund.
  • Once supplier confirms refund, process refund to the customer.

C. Disputes Filed or Threatened to File Dispute

  • Cancel only if the customer has filed or threatened to file a dispute for any of the following:
    • Loss in transit items
    • Unfulfilled orders
    • Resent damaged items
  • Always confirm the dispute has been filed or the threat has been clearly communicated before canceling.

    • 3. Cancellation Restrictions (When NOT to cancel)

    Orders that are:

    • Fulfilled, in transit, or delivered
    • The original purchase that was placed along with an Aftersell (discounted add-on) item, and the customer wishes to retain only the Aftersell item—even if the request is made within 3 hours and the Aftersell item is not yet fulfilled.

    Orders with disputes filed but:

    • Still within delivery timeframe (5-12 business days)
    • Disputed due to delayed orders but are still moving

    🚨 Order Status Workflow

    1. Unfulfilled for More Than 1 Day

    Issue: Order has not been processed; no tracking provided.

    ✅ Action:

    • Follow up with the supplier.
    • Inform the customer:
      “We’re checking with our fulfillment team regarding your order status and will update you shortly.”

    2. Info Received for More Than 3 Days

    Issue: Tracking created but no shipment movement.

    ✅ Action:

    • Follow up with the supplier.
    • Inform the customer:
      “Your order is next in line for checkout, and we sincerely apologize for the delay. We’ve been experiencing unusually high demand for these items, and our fulfillment team is working hard to process all orders as quickly as possible. We truly appreciate your patience and will keep you updated as soon as your package is on the way.”

    3. Error in Tracking

    Issue: Invalid or broken tracking link/number.

    ✅ Action:

    • Ask the supplier to fix or correct tracking info.
    • If you have platform access, assist the supplier in updating the tracking number.

    4. No Movement for More Than 5 Days in Transit (Possible Lost in Transit)

    Issue: Package stuck in the same location for over 5 days.

    ✅ Action:

    • Follow up with the supplier and request a resend.
    • Inform the customer:
      “It looks like your order may have been delayed in transit, and we sincerely apologize for the inconvenience. We’re currently following up with both our fulfillment team and the courier to get a clearer update. If your order remains undelivered beyond the standard 5–12 business day delivery window, we’d be happy to offer you a 40% discount on a future purchase as a gesture of goodwill. Thank you for your patience — we’ll keep you posted as soon as we have more information”
    • If the customer has filed or threatened a dispute, refer to the Dispute Management Workflow.

    5. In Transit but Moving (Delayed)

    Issue: Package is delayed but tracking shows movement.

    ✅ Action:

    • If within delivery timeframe (5-12 business days): advise the customer to wait.
    • If outside delivery timeframe: offer up to 40% discount for the delay.

    6. Returned to Sender

    Issue: Package is being returned to the supplier.

    ✅ Action:

    • Ask the supplier to resend the package.
    • If due to incorrect/incomplete address, contact the customer for the correct address.

    7. Delivered but Not Received

    Issue: Tracking shows "delivered" but customer didn’t receive the item.

    ✅ Action:

    • Request Proof of Delivery (POD) from the supplier.
    • Advise the customer to:
      • Check with neighbors, building staff, or household members.
      • Wait up to 3 business days, as items may still arrive.
    • If POD is provided but the customer insists they didn’t receive it:
      • Ask the customer to obtain a proof of loss from the courier.
      • Once provided, proceed to resend the item.

    Recipient moved or refused delivery Customer moved or declined the package.

    Issue: Courier has marked the package as lost.

    ✅ Action:

    • Inform the customer:

    9. Failed Delivery Attempt

    Issue: Courier failed to deliver the package due to:

    • Recipient unavailable
    • Incorrect/incomplete address
    • Delivery refused
    • Restricted access
    • Weather or operational issues

    ✅ Action:

    • Notify the customer of the failed attempt.
    • Ask for any needed information (e.g., correct address or delivery instructions).
    • No refunds should be processed.
    • Resend the item once the issue is resolved.

    ❗ Important Note:

    • For refused deliveries or orders not picked up, no refund should be issued.
    • Resend the item once the issue is clarified.

    🧭 Customer Issue Resolution Workflow

    1. Quality Concerns

    Definition: Customer feels the item does not meet expectations in terms of look, material, or overall feel.
    Important: These are subjective concerns and are not valid for supplier reshipment requests.

    • ❌ Do not ask for photos
    • ✅ Acknowledge and apologize for not meeting their expectations
    • 🤝 Offer resolution on a case-by-case basis:
      • → Temp check the customer’s tone and response first
      • → If the customer seems mildly disappointed, offer a partial refund or store credit (especially if they prefer to return)
      • → If the customer is not very upset, you may offer a replacement with fee
    • 🗂️ Do not submit to supplier — this type of issue does not qualify for supplier reshipment.

    2. Sizing Does Not Fit

    Definition: Customer says the item does not fit properly.

    • 📏 Check if the item comes in other sizes
      • → If available: offer replacement with reshipment fee
      • → If not: offer different item replacement (also with reshipment fee)
    • 💬 Clearly communicate that reshipment fee applies
    • 🧾 Follow up with correct invoice and shipping instructions

    3. Damaged, Missing, or Incorrect Items

    Definition: Jewelry item is damaged, incorrect, or something from the order is missing.

    • 📸 Request clear photos or videos from the customer showing the issue
    • 👁️ Review and confirm the issue yourself as a support rep (no need to wait for customer to say it’s wrong — you decide if valid)
    • 📩 Once confirmed on your end, submit the case to the supplier for reshipment
    • 🔁 Request reshipment from the supplier
    • 🧾 Wait for supplier to provide tracking or confirmation of the new shipment

    4. Address or Item Change

    Definition: Customer wants to change shipping address or ordered item.

    • 📦 If order is unfulfilled: update directly in the system
    • 🚚 If order is fulfilled: request change from supplier (Discord)
    • ✅ Confirm with customer once change is made

    5. Return to Sender (RTS)

    Definition: Parcel is returned by the courier.

    • 🔍 Check reason for RTS
    • 🤝 Coordinate with supplier for reshipment
    • 📩 Inform customer that we will resend once items are received by warehouse

    6. Delivered But Not Received

    Definition: Order marked as delivered, but customer didn’t get it.

    • 📦 Request proof of delivery from supplier
    • 📤 Share the proof with the customer
    • 📄 If still not received: request proof of loss from customer (e.g., official documentation from the courier)
    • 🔁 Once obtained, offer free reshipment

    7. Delayed Orders

    Definition: Order taking longer than expected to arrive.

    • ⏱️ Refer to Delayed Orders Workflow
    • 💬 Keep customer informed with tracking updates and delivery expectations
    • 🎯 Only offer resolution (refund/reshipment) if time exceeds stated limits and courier shows no progress
